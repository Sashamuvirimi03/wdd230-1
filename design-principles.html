Proximity

Nike

Nike.com

Nike's website effectively uses the proximity principle by grouping related elements together. Navigation, product categories, and call-to-action buttons are strategically placed for a seamless user experience.

Nike, Inc., is a multinational corporation known for its athletic footwear, apparel, and accessories. Founded in 1964, Nike has become a global leader in the sports industry. The company's mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world.

Not only recognized for its cutting-edge products, but Nike is also renowned for its impactful marketing campaigns and sponsorships with top athletes. The iconic "Just Do It" slogan is synonymous with the brand's commitment to empowering individuals to push their limits.
